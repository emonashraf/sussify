Starting a campaign is simple and user-friendly. Just click the “Start a Campaign” button, sign up or log in to your account, and fill in the required details like your campaign title, story, funding goal, and an engaging image. Once submitted, our team will review your campaign to ensure it meets our guidelines, and then it will go live. You can begin sharing your campaign immediately to attract support.

Your donation goes directly to the campaign organizer, less any applicable transaction and platform fees. We ensure that all funds are securely transferred to the beneficiaries through verified payment gateways. Additionally, we work closely with campaign owners to ensure transparency, so donors can feel confident that their contributions are truly making a difference.
